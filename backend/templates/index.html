GreatDay

The simple, intuitive app that makes it easy to keep track of the positive activities that occur in your daily life to help promote a happier lifestyle.

  • Iphone
  • Android
  • Windows

Improve your mental wellness

By associating emotions with different activities and different days, you can look back at your previous days history and notice little things that make your day better. Having this physical visualization can be helpful in bringing you a better tomorrow.

"Ever since I started using GreatDay, I have been more mindful of the things that make me happy. I can focus more on the things that make my daily life more positive."

  • Kevin Zheng, Software Engineer

Track Your Growth

GreatDay provides insights into activities that make you the most happy, bring you the most sadness, and keeps track of the frequencies of these activites. Visualizations such as bar graphs and line graphs help you physically see your progress.

"GreatDay has improved my life drastically. I stopped being so focusing so much on the negative aspects of my life and I started doing more of the things that make me happy."

  • Salman Siraj, Entrepreneur
